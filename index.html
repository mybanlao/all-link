A Classic Parisian Cafe

Entering Cafe 1960 is like traveling back in time. The setting, which has opulent chandeliers that display the expertly hand-crafted confections, pairs nicely with the delicious delicacies from Chef Jeremi of France. People come here to enjoy a coffee and the ambiance.
